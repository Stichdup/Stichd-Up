How It Works

Three steps.
One perfect wrap.

1
Design Your Wrap
Tell us your vehicle's make, model, and year. Pick your material — leather, suede, or Alcantara. Choose your colours, stitching style, centre markers, and thumb grips. The whole thing takes about 5 minutes.
2
Handcrafted to Order
Your wrap is hand-cut and stitched using automotive-grade materials. Every single one is made specifically for your car — no generic templates, no mass production. Ready in 10–12 business days.
3
Fitted or Shipped
Melbourne customers can book our mobile fitting service — we come to you and fit it in 1–2 hours. DIY kit customers get full step-by-step instructions and remote support via phone or video call.
